Odisha Gazette Notification Process ଓଡିଶା ଗେଜେଟ୍ ବିଜ୍ଞପ୍ତି ପ୍ରକ୍ରିୟା

Understanding the 'Why' and 'How' of Gazette Notifications in Odisha.

Why is Gazette Notification Needed in Odisha? ଓଡିଶାରେ ଗେଜେଟ୍ ବିଜ୍ଞପ୍ତି କାହିଁକି ଆବଶ୍ୟକ?

The Odisha Gazette is an official public journal of the Government of Odisha. A notification published in it is a legal requirement and proof for various purposes, including:

  • Name Change: Legally changing your first name, middle name, or surname. (ନାମ ପରିବର୍ତ୍ତନ)
  • Surname Change After Marriage: For women adopting their husband's surname or adding it. (ବିବାହ ପରେ ସାଙ୍ଗିଆ ପରିବର୍ତ୍ତନ)
  • Change of Religion: Official declaration of conversion to a different religion. (ଧର୍ମ ପରିବର୍ତ୍ତନ)
  • Correction in Name/Birth Date in Documents: Sometimes required as supporting proof. (କାଗଜପତ୍ରରେ ନାମ/ଜନ୍ମ ତାରିଖ ସଂଶୋଧନ)
  • Public Notices & Other Legal Declarations: For various official and legal announcements. (ସାର୍ବଜନୀନ ବିଜ୍ଞପ୍ତି ଏବଂ ଅନ୍ୟାନ୍ୟ ଆଇନଗତ ଘୋଷଣା)

General Steps for Odisha Gazette Notification (Roadmap):

1Affidavit Preparation (ଶପଥପତ୍ର ପ୍ରସ୍ତୁତି)

Draft an affidavit on appropriate non-judicial stamp paper. It must clearly state your old details, new details, and the reason for the change. This affidavit must be attested by a Notary Public or a Judicial Magistrate.

2Newspaper Publication (ଖବରକାଗଜରେ ପ୍ରକାଶନ)

Publish an advertisement about your intended change in at least two local daily newspapers: one in Odia and one in English, having wide circulation in Odisha. Keep original copies of these newspaper pages.

3Application Form & Document Compilation (ଆବେଦନ ଫର୍ମ ଓ କାଗଜପତ୍ର)

Obtain the prescribed application form from the Directorate of Printing, Stationery and Publication, Odisha, Cuttack. Compile all documents: original affidavit, newspaper cuttings, ID proof, address proof, photos, and a request letter.

4Submission & Fees (ଦାଖଲ ଏବଂ ଶୁଳ୍କ)

Submit the complete application set along with the prescribed fees (usually via Demand Draft) to the Directorate of Printing, Stationery and Publication, Odisha, Cuttack. (Verify the current address and fee details).

5Verification & Publication (ଯାଞ୍ଚ ଓ ପ୍ରକାଶନ)

The authorities will verify your documents. Upon successful verification, your notification will be published in an upcoming issue of the Odisha Gazette.

6Gazette Copy Collection (ଗେଜେଟ୍ କପି ସଂଗ୍ରହ)

Once published, you can obtain official copies of the Gazette containing your notification. The process for collection may vary.

Please Note: This is a general guide. The exact procedures, required documents, and fees can be subject to change by the Odisha Government. We help you navigate the current and correct process.
